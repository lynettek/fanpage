Biography

Death Cab for Cutie is a pop/ rock band that formed in Washington in 1997. Band members include Ben Gibbard, Chris Walla, Nick Harmer, Jason McGerr, Michael Schorr and Nathan Good. The band signed a contract in 1998 with Seattle based Barsuk Records, and produced their first album titled "Something About Airplanes". After some commercial success, they went on to sign a contract with Atlantic records in 2004. Their music has been featured on the television show "The O.C."
